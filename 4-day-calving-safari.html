4-Day Southern Serengeti & Ngorongoro Safari

Experience the wonder of the Great Migration's calving season on this 4-day safari to Tanzania's Southern Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater. Witness thousands of wildebeest giving birth on the nutrient-rich Ndutu Plains, creating one of nature's most spectacular events.

This compact yet immersive safari offers incredible opportunities to observe newborn calves taking their first steps, predator-prey interactions, and the diverse wildlife of the Ngorongoro Crater. With expert guides and carefully selected accommodations, you'll experience the raw beauty of Tanzania's most famous ecosystems in a shorter timeframe.

Day 1: Arrive Arusha

You will arrive at JRO (Kilimanjaro International Airport) where you will be warmly welcomed by your driver guide. Afterward, you will be transferred to your accommodation in Arusha, where you can enjoy dinner and relax overnight. A pre-safari briefing will also be held to prepare you for the adventure ahead.

Day 2: Arusha – Southern Serengeti

Early in the morning, after breakfast at your hotel in Arusha, you will be transferred to Arusha Airport for your scheduled flight to the Ndutu area, located within the Ngorongoro Conservation Area at the southern edge of the Serengeti ecosystem. The drive from your hotel to Arusha Airport is approximately 8 km and takes about 20 minutes depending on traffic. Upon arrival at Ndutu Airstrip (flight time is approximately 1 hour), you will be warmly welcomed by your professional driver-guide and begin your first exciting game drive in the southern Serengeti. This region is particularly famous between December and March as it hosts part of the Great Migration, with massive herds of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles giving birth and grazing on the nutrient-rich plains. You will spend the day exploring the Ndutu plains and surrounding woodlands, observing wildlife in their natural habitat and capturing stunning views of the vast savannah. In the late afternoon, you'll drive to your specially arranged safari camp for some well-deserved relaxation. Enjoy a delicious dinner under the stars followed by an overnight stay in the heart of the wilderness.

Estimated driving distance: 30–80 km depending on wildlife movement
Game viewing duration: 4–6 hours

Day 3: Southern Serengeti

Wake up surrounded by the breathtaking wilderness of the southern Serengeti's Ndutu area! After an early breakfast, you'll set off for another exciting day of game drives. During this time of year, the Ndutu plains are lush and green, providing fresh grazing grounds for the massive herds of wildebeest. Many of them remain here, feeding on the rich grasses and continuing to give birth to their young. This period is not only crucial for the survival of the wildebeest calves but also a spectacular sight, as the landscape is dotted with new life. Predators are ever-present, aware that this is a time of abundance, and you may witness dramatic scenes of survival and instinct. The cycle of life plays out vividly here, making the Ndutu area one of the best spots to witness nature at its rawest. You'll carry packed lunch to maximize your time in the bush, continuing to explore different areas throughout the day. In the late afternoon, return to your lodge/camp for dinner and another overnight stay under the Serengeti skies.

Day 4: Southern Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater – Arusha

After an early breakfast at your camp in the southern Serengeti (Ndutu area), you will check out and begin your journey toward the world-renowned Ngorongoro Crater. The drive from Ndutu to the crater rim takes approximately 80 km and around 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on wildlife sightings and road conditions. Upon arrival at the Ngorongoro Crater, you'll descend 600 meters into this ancient volcanic caldera for a half-day game drive. The crater floor offers a unique and concentrated wildlife viewing experience, home to a dense population of animals including elephants, lions, hyenas, zebras, buffaloes, and the rare black rhino. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is often referred to as "Africa's Garden of Eden" for its breathtaking scenery and rich biodiversity. After an incredible morning of wildlife viewing, you'll ascend back to the crater rim and begin your return journey to Arusha. The drive from Ngorongoro Crater to Arusha is approximately 190 km and takes around 4.5 to 5 hours. Upon arrival in Arusha in the late afternoon or early evening, you'll be transferred to your accommodation or to the airport, depending on your onward travel plans.
