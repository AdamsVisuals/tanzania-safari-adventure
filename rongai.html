Rongai Route Overview

The most scenic and less-traveled path to Uhuru Peak with wilderness camping

The Rongai Route is the only trail that approaches Kilimanjaro from the north, near the Kenyan border. This less-traveled path offers a true wilderness experience with spectacular views and abundant wildlife sightings. The route features a gradual ascent profile, making it excellent for acclimatization with a high success rate. Unlike the busier southern routes, Rongai provides a more remote and serene climbing experience through pristine forests and alpine meadows before joining the Marangu Route for the final ascent to the summit.

6-7 Days Recommended
80% Success Rate
73km Round Trip
5,895m Summit Altitude
