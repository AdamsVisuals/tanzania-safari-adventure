The Great River Crossing

Experience the most dramatic event in the animal kingdom

Between July and October, witness the breathtaking Mara River crossing where over 1.5 million wildebeest and zebra brave crocodile-infested waters in their relentless migration cycle. This heart-pounding spectacle features massive herds gathering at riverbanks, dramatic leaps into turbulent waters, and nature's raw drama unfolding as predators await their opportunity. It's a once-in-a-lifetime experience that showcases the ultimate survival story.
