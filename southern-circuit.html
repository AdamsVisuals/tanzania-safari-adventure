Southern Circuit Safaris

Experience Tanzania's wild and remote safari route

The Southern Circuit offers a more exclusive and off-the-beaten-path safari experience. With vast wilderness areas, lower tourist numbers, and diverse ecosystems, this region includes spectacular destinations like Selous Game Reserve, Ruaha National Park, Mikumi National Park, and Udzungwa Mountains. Discover pristine landscapes, exceptional wildlife viewing, and authentic wilderness adventures.

Southern Circuit Parks & Reserves

Discover Tanzania's wild and remote safari destinations

Selous Game Reserve

One of Africa's largest game reserves with diverse wildlife and boat safaris on the Rufiji River.

Ruaha National Park

Tanzania's largest national park known for its large elephant herds and dramatic landscapes.

Mikumi National Park

Often called 'mini-Serengeti' for its abundant wildlife and accessibility from Dar es Salaam.

Udzungwa Mountains National Park

Known as the 'Galapagos of Africa' for its biodiversity and spectacular waterfalls.

Kitulo National Park

A botanical paradise known as 'God's Garden' with spectacular wildflower displays.

Mufindi Highlands

Cool highland scenery, tea estates, and forest walks in Tanzania’s southern uplands.
