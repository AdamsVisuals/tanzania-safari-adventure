Eastern Circuit Safaris

Where the beach meets the bush in Tanzania's unique coastal safari experience

The Eastern Circuit offers a distinctive blend of coastal beauty, cultural richness, and wildlife encounters. This region features Tanzania's only coastal national park, Saadani, where you can see animals with the ocean as a backdrop. Explore the biodiversity of Mkomazi National Park, hike the lush Usambara Mountains, and discover the historical Swahili coast. The Eastern Circuit provides a perfect combination of beach relaxation and safari adventure.

Eastern Circuit Parks & Destinations

Discover Tanzania's unique coastal and cultural safari destinations

Saadani National Park

Tanzania's only coastal national park where beach meets bush, offering unique wildlife viewing with ocean backdrop.

Mkomazi National Park

A vital sanctuary for black rhinos and wild dogs with stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro and the Pare Mountains.

Usambara Mountains

Lush green mountains with hiking trails through traditional villages, tea plantations, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Pangani

A laid-back coastal town with palm-fringed beaches, Swahili culture, and a rich history as a former trading port.

Tanga

A historic coastal city known for its colonial architecture, bustling port, and gateway to the Swahili coast’s cultural heritage.

Zanzibar Island

The spice island with pristine beaches, historic Stone Town, and rich cultural heritage (often combined with Eastern Circuit safaris).
